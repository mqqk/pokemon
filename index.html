Introducing Trevenant!

Bio Evolution Possible Moves

Trevenant (a.k.a the Elder Tree Pokemon) is a Ghost-Grass type located in the Kalos mountain region. Using its roots, Trevenant can control the trees around it to protect its forest home. Smaller Pokemon sometimes live in its hollow body.

Trevenant evolves from Phantump. It stands 4'11" with a weight of 156.5 pounds.

This 1'04" tall and 15.4 pound Pokemon is found in old tree trunks far away from people. You'll have to be quiet and sneaky to find this lonely Pokemon.

The possible moves Trevenant is expected to have are as follows.

  • Horn Leech
  • Tackle
  • Confuse Ray
  • Astonish

There are plenty more, but you'll have to find one to learn more!
